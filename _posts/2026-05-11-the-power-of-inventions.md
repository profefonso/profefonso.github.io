![What is an invention?]({{ '/assets/images/invention_concept.png' | relative_url }})

An invention is a creation made by humans to solve problems, make tasks easier, or improve daily life. Inventions have changed the way we live, work, and communicate.

### Characteristics of inventions

* They arise from a need or problem.
* They seek to make activities easier.
* They can be simple or highly technological.
* They help the development of society.
* They evolve over time thanks to new ideas.

---

## What are ancient inventions?

Ancient inventions are creations made many centuries ago, before modern technology. They were created to solve basic needs such as transportation, writing, measuring time, or building tools.

![Ancient vs Modern]({{ '/assets/images/ancient_vs_modern.png' | relative_url }})

### Characteristics
* They were made with simple materials.
* They were developed without electricity or digital technology.
* Many are still important today.
* They marked the beginning of human progress.

### Examples
- **The wheel**: It was one of the most important inventions in history. It allowed the transportation of people and objects more quickly.
- **Paper**: Created in ancient China, it made writing and preserving information easier.
- **The compass**: It helped navigators find directions and discover new routes.
- **The hourglass**: It was used to measure time before modern clocks.

---

## What are modern inventions?

Modern inventions are recent creations that use science and advanced technology to improve communication, medicine, transportation, and other areas of life.

### Characteristics
* They use electricity, the internet, or digital systems.
* They are faster and more efficient.
* They are constantly updated.
* They make daily life and work easier.

### Examples
- **The smartphone**: It allows people to communicate, browse the internet, and use applications from anywhere.
- **The computer**: It helps with work, studies, research, and entertainment.
- **Solar panels**: They transform solar energy into electricity and help protect the environment.
- **The 3D printer**: It can create physical objects from digital designs.

---

## To what extent can inventions be categorized?

Inventions can be classified according to different aspects:
* **By period**: ancient, modern, and contemporary.
* **By function**: communication, transportation, medicine, education, entertainment, etc.
* **By technology**: mechanical, electrical, digital, or intelligent.
* **By impact**: inventions that changed history or inventions for everyday use.

---

## The printing press

![The Printing Press]({{ '/assets/images/printing_press.png' | relative_url }})

The printing press was an invention created in the 15th century by Johannes Gutenberg. Its main function was to copy books and texts quickly, something that previously had to be done by hand and took a long time.

**Thanks to the printing press:**
* Books became more accessible.
* More people learned to read and study.
* Knowledge and ideas spread throughout the world.
* It helped the progress of education and science.

It is considered one of the most important inventions in history because it changed the way people shared information. Thanks to the printing press, knowledge reached more people and access to education and reading increased around the world.

---

## Conclusion

Ancient and modern inventions have been fundamental to the progress of humanity. Each creation reflects the human ability to innovate and find solutions that improve our daily lives.
